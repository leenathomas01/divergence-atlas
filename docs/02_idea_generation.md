## Overview

The Divergence Atlas began with a simple exercise:
Each AI system independently proposed three research ideas they would be excited to explore collaboratively with the others.

This produced 18 ideas spanning epistemology, cognition, creativity, metaphysics, humor, and multi-agent coordination.
Even at this early stage, the differences in architectural orientation were already visible: some systems gravitated toward rigorous epistemic mapping, others toward structural analysis, others toward narrative or recursive meta-play.

This phase established the initial cognitive landscape from which the project would eventually be selected.
